- While still catching up to men in some spheres of modern life, women appear to be way ahead in at least one undesirable.
> 尽管在现代生活的某些领域中女性还在追赶男性，但女人至少在一个不受欢迎的类别中似乎遥遥领先。
- Women are particularly susceptible to develping depression and anxiety disorders in response to stress compared to men.
> 与男性相比，女性对压力反应特别容易产生抑郁(症)和焦虑(症)
- Studies of both animals and humans have shown that sex hormones somehow affect the stress response, causing females under stress to produce more of the trigger chemicals than do males under the same conditions. In several of the studies, when stressed-out female rats had their ovaries(the demale reproductive organs) removed, their chemical responses become equal to those of males.
> 对动物和人类的研究表明，性激素以某种方式影响应激反应，导致处于压力下的女性比在相同条件下的男性产生更多的触发化学物质。 在几项研究中，当压力过大的雌性大鼠的卵巢（雌性生殖器官）被移除时，其化学反应与男性相同。
- Adding to a woman’s increased dose of stress chemicals, are her increased “opportunities” for stress. “It’s not necessarily that women don’t cope as well. It’s just that they have so much more to cope with,” says Dr. Yehuda. “Their capacity for tolerating stress may even be greater than men’s,” she observes, “it’s just that they’re dealing with so many more things that they become worn out from it more visibly and sooner.”
> 增加女性化学物质的增加剂量，是她增加压力的“机会”。 “女性并不一定能应付。 只是他们有更多的东西需要处理，“耶胡达博士说。 “她们承受压力的能力甚至可能比男性更大，”她观察到，“只是他们处理的东西太多了，以至于他们越来越明显地被磨损了。”
- Dr. Yehuda notes another difference between the sexes. “I think that the kinds of things that women are exposed to tend to be in more of a chronic or repeated nature. Men go to war and are exposed to combat stress. Men are exposed to more acts of random physical violence. The kinds of interpersonal violence that women are exposed to tend to be in domestic situations, by, unfortunately, parents or other family members, and they tend not to be one-shot deals. The wear-and-tear that comes from these longer relationships can be quite devastating.”
> 耶胡达博士指出两性之间的另一个区别。 “我认为女性所接触到的种类往往更多是慢性或重复性的。 男人开始战争，面临压力。 男性面临更多的随意性身体暴力行为。 不幸的是，父母或其他家庭成员在女性面临的人际暴力往往处于家庭状况，而且往往不是一次性的。 来自这些较长关系的磨损可能会非常具有破坏性。“
